# Shein Magento (Adobe Commerce) Connect by M2E

Shein Magento (Adobe Commerce) Connect by M2E is a trusted Magento extension that enables merchants of all sizes to fully integrate their Magento-based systems with the Shein marketplace.

The extension makes it easy to upload your Magento inventory to Shein. It provides automatic inventory and order synchronization, keeping your product and order information up-to-date on Magento and Shein.

Here's how else Shein Magento Connect by M2E can benefit your business:

- List your Magento products on Shein in several clicks
- Customize your Magento product settings specifically for Shein
- Enjoy real-time, automated synchronization for prices and quantities
- Manage Shein orders directly in Magento

Check out our [user documentation](https://docs-m2.m2epro.com/docs-category/shein-magento-adobe-commerce-integration/) to learn more about the extension.
